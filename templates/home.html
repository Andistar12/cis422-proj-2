Welcome to tac



What is tac?

Organizing a family BBQ, a bicycle race, or any other social event? Need to send important information to your group/community? tac is the place where you can do all that in a new and accessible way. tac works by giving individuals the ability to create and join boards, which represent communities they want to be a part of. tac users can then make posts on the boards they are subscribed to, and upvote existing posts there. If a given post meets a certain threshold of upvotes, then everyone subscribed to that board receives a push notification about the post.

How to use tac

Start using tac today by creating an account using the register button at the top of the page. There is no need to provide any personal information - you can be completely anonymous if you so choose. Once you have registered, log into your account and find some boards you want to join. No boards suit your interests? Create a new board using the "Create Board" button in the navigation bar at the top of the page. When you have a board you have joined or created, click on "My Boards" in the navigation bar and then go to a board of your choice to create a post, or view and upvote other posts.
