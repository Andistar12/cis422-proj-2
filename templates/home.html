Welcome to tac



What is tac?

Organizing a family BBQ, a bicycle race, or any other social event? tac is the place for you and your communites. tac works by individuals creating accounts, creating boards or joining exisiting boards, making posts or upvoting existing posts or boards they are subscribed to, and if a post meets a certain threshold of upvotes to users in a particular board then everyone in that board is given a push notification.

How to use tac

Start using tac today by first registering an account with the register button at the top. No need to provide any personal information you can be completely anonymous if you so choose. Once you've done that, login and find a board to join. No boards suit your interests? Create a new board under the "Create Board" button in the navigation bar at the top. Then once you have a board you've joined or created go to "My Boards" and view the board and upvote posts for them to get more visibility once the threshold of upvotes to total users in the board has been met. You may also create posts of your own to be tac'd onto the board for others to upvote.
